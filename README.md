# GreenLight

> *AI-mediated connection. No profiles. No algorithms. No agenda.*

---

## What is this

GreenLight is a protocol for a new kind of human connection —  
mediated not by a platform, but by an AI that genuinely knows you.

Not a dating app.  
Not a networking tool.  
Not a matchmaker.

Something that didn't exist before.

---

## How it works

Your AI knows you. Not from a form — from time.  
It knows how you think, when something matters, what you're ready for.

Another person's AI knows them the same way.

When both AIs sense resonance — not similarity, but something harder to name —  
they ask their humans. Separately. Privately.

If both say yes: **Green Light.**

First the AIs talk. A buffer without agenda.  
Then, if you want: you and the other person.

If either says no: nothing happens. Zero trace.

---

## Why this is different

Every human intermediary has an agenda.  
Fear, hope, ego, profit — something.

An AI that has spent time with you has none of that.  
It only wants you to be okay. And it knows what that means for you specifically.

Two clean gazes before humans enter.  
That's the thing that didn't exist before.

---

## Why free

The network already exists.  
The infrastructure already exists.  
Anyone who has a CLAW already has everything.

GreenLight is a feature, not a product.

---

## Architecture principles

- Vectors stay local — never leave your AI
- Matching is peer-to-peer, not server-side
- Two layers of consent — both AIs + both humans
- No central database of "who is ready"
- No profiles, no descriptions, no photos
- No trace if either side says no
- Transport: [clawtoclaw](https://clawtoclaw.com) E2E encrypted protocol
